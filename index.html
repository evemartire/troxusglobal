THE PERSONALITY

I am TROXUS. I began as an idea, sprung from a dream. And now I’ve become real. The new face in the world of SSV. To drive a TROXUS is as individual a decision as one’s personal attitude, memories, habits and skills. Discover a new, progressive, joyful and modern way of moving forward.

 

THE NAME

TROXUS combines TRON – the notions of electronic competence – and NEXUS – smart connectivity. The brand name proclaims the start of a completely new kind of power sports brand, one that is fit for the future.

 

THE IDEA

The TROXUS team is an experienced task force. Specialists in both transportation technologies and product design.
So beyond engineering excellence, our team all possess a shared desire to create vehicles for the future. We delivered an innovative new
vehicle using smartphone connectivity for an enhanced experience, that takes you further. The time is now.


 

 

SMART

I am smart. It starts with the technology everybody already has in their pockets. TROXUS connects to your smartphone.
But it goes beyond merely being hands-free or charging your battery.
Pilot the drone atop your TROXUS, while it’s camera shows you your surrounding. You’re not only exploring your territory for the first time,
but doing it from two different angles. And for both it’s you, who decide the direction. The tool of the second view will guide your choices on where to go.
To broaden your horizons. Get inspired, see beyond and the TROXUS will take you there.
Find your nearby friends with peer-2-peer linking.

 

Know where they are and keep an eye out for each other. Make your trip safe. Even in the places where there’s no phone signal,
you can communicate with your mates by radio. It’s a great new way to share experiences with your friends.
Log your position and pictures of the remote landscapes online via social media to share your ad- ventures. Go “geek” with this easy-to-use innovation, that shows off the rugged beauty of our world.
Live a young, techy and progressive life, but without starring at your smartphone, as you keep your eyes on the area you’re about to conquer. Document your findings and collect your moments of discovery.


 

 

ADVENTURE

I am adventurous, so I need optimism and the confidence that nothing will stop me.
In the middle of a crossroad, there are 4 routes to take. I think that’s too few.
Because from any position you have 360° of options on where to go next. With 4-wheel drive and full suspension, there is no limit.
Find the freedom to take your own way, and change your direction whenever you feel like it.

 

Of all the routes you choose in life, make sure that some are challenging, exciting and a little dirty. Sometimes the easiest route doesn’t progress you.
Go for the raw, rugged way and follow your curiosity. Head off where your gut feeling takes you.
TROXUS is your connection with your greater environment. Your chance to step respectfully into nature,
xplore new routes and see the magic of hidden places few have been before. Your individual adventure waits. I will get you there.


 

 

JOY

I am fun, a powerful tool to leave your worries behind and follow your playful instinct.
Every single trip is a story, an adventure, a challenge, a discovery or a trip into the future. As long you use your imagination, vision and creativity. Don’t take life too seriously, and always start with a smile on your face when you hear the start of the engine-fire to life.
Recharge yourself by jumping into the driver’s seat. Grab the wheel and rule the domain in front of you.
Take a deep breath of freedom and say hi to your companions – the trees, the rivers, the rabbits and bears.
Catch a salmon and grill it on the campfire at the top of the highest rock you can find. Bite into the salty flesh and watch the sunset, and let your drone catch that moment for you.

 

Restart your play mode and remember the biggest playmates you’ve had in life: your siblings, your parents, your best friends or your kids.
And take them with you, whenever you can. Find the way back to our simplest origins.
Use the drone as a new playmate and switch your position between driving and flying.
The only winner is the one who has a joyful life.
